# amazon_sales_performance

📊 Project Overview
The objective of this dashboard was to transform raw Amazon product data into meaningful business insights that can help stakeholders make informed decisions regarding product performance, pricing, and customer satisfaction.

🔑 Key Business Metrics
✅ Total Products: 1,406
✅ Estimated Revenue: 70.05M
✅ Average Product Rating: 4.10 / 5
✅ Average Discount Offered: 47%
✅ Total Customer Savings: 3.05M

📈 Key Insights
🔹 Electronics dominates the marketplace
Nearly 476 products belong to the Electronics category.
Electronics contributes approximately 81.9% of the total estimated revenue (~57.34M), making it the strongest revenue-generating category.
🔹 Top Categories
Electronics
Home & Kitchen
Computers & Accessories
These three categories account for the majority of products listed and overall sales.
🔹 Customer Satisfaction
Average product rating across all categories is 4.10, indicating generally positive customer feedback.
Categories with higher rating counts also demonstrate stronger customer engagement.
🔹 Discount Strategy
The average discount offered is 47%.
Medium to high discount ranges contribute significantly to overall sales, suggesting competitive pricing plays an important role in customer purchasing decisions.
Interestingly, products with minimal discounts still maintain strong customer ratings, indicating that product quality influences customer satisfaction beyond pricing.
🔹 Savings Analysis
Customers collectively saved approximately 3.05M through discounts, highlighting Amazon's aggressive pricing strategy.
